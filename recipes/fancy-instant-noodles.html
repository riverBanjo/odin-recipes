Fancy Instant Noodles

Description

We've all been there. If you haven't been there yet there will come a day when you really don't have the energy to make a good meal. However, you know you can't just eat instant noodles and hope for the best. You do still need to have some nourishment.

Fancifying your instant noodles is nothing new. People do it all the time. This recipe is just how I do it to maximize the laziness, keep it cheap, keep it flavorful, and keep myself nourished.

Ingredients

  • 1â€‰pack of instant noodles. (I've found I really enjoy indomie instant fried noodles)
  • 1 Green onion stalk chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 small can of chicken
  • 1 small can of bean sprouts

Steps

  1. Boil some water on the stove with an egg in it. I typically soft boil my egg which takes around 5 - 6 minutes. Otherwise, consult the internet for your boiling time.
  2. While your egg is boiling you can follow the instructions on your noodles to combine sauces and seasonings in a bowl.
  3. Remove egg from boiling water and place in a ice bath. Make sure to crack the egg in the cold water as much as possible to avoid the shell pulling peices of the egg white off.
  4. Add your noodle block, bean sprouts, and canned chicken to the boiling water and cook for recommended noodle boil time. Typically 3 minutes.
  5. While your ingredients cook you can deshell your egg.
  6. Once the noodles and others are done you can strain them and add them to your seasonings.
  7. Cut egg in half and lay ontop of your noodle bed.
  8. Garnish with your green onion.
